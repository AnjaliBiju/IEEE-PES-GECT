IEEE

The IEEE is a global organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. With over 400,000 members in 160+ countries, IEEE fosters innovation, sets industry standards, and publishes high-quality research.

It supports professionals through technical societies, conferences, and educational initiatives in fields like AI, robotics, and renewable energy.

Learn more at www.ieee.org.

IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES)

The IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES) focuses on advancing electrical power and energy engineering. It promotes research, industry collaboration, and the development of standards for power systems, renewable energy, and energy efficiency.

With 42,000+ members, PES provides networking opportunities, organizes global conferences, and offers educational resources, scholarships, and industry publications.

Learn more at www.ieee-pes.org.
